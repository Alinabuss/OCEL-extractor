Little Mountain Fire as of 01/15/2025 at 6:54 PM:
Firefighters and law enforcement responded at 2 p.m. today (1/15) to a fire burning in the brush near Little Moutain Drive near Edge Hill Drive. Thanks to a multi-engine and handcrew response, the San Bernardino Fire Department is reporting via social media that the forward progress of the fire has been stopped.Firefighters will continue gaining containment of the fire and then transition into mop-up activities. All CAL FIRE resources have been released. Little Moutain Road remains closed.The San Bernardino Police Department has arrested an adult male in connection with the incident, according to the department via social media.
